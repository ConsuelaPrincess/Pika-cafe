NOTICE

  • Order Lead Time: All custom cake orders must be placed at least two weeks in advance to ensure availability and quality.
  • Deposit Requirement: A non-refundable deposit of 50% of the total cost is required at the time of order placement.
  • Final Payment: The remaining balance is due upon pickup or delivery of the cake.
  • Design Changes: Any changes to the design or details of the custom cake must be made at least one week prior to the delivery date.
  • Cancellation Policy: Cancellations must be made at least one week in advance for a partial refund. Cancellations made less than one week in advance will forfeit the deposit.
  • Allergy Information: Customers are responsible for informing us of any allergies or dietary restrictions at the time of ordering.
  • Tasting Sessions: Complimentary tasting sessions are available for orders over a certain amount. Please inquire for details.
  • Delivery Fee: A delivery fee will be applied to orders requiring delivery. The fee varies based on the distance from our café.
  • Pickup Time: Custom cakes must be picked up during our regular business hours unless special arrangements have been made.
  • Storage and Handling: We are not responsible for the quality of the cake if it is not stored or handled properly after pickup or delivery.
