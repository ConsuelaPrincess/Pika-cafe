About Us

As night descends and the city's hustle gradually fades, a hidden gem awaits at the corner: a retro Showa-era cafe and bar that only opens its doors after dusk, resembling a tunnel through time. Stepping into this unique space, you're transported back to the nostalgic atmosphere of Japan's Showa period. The vintage furnishings and elegant wooden bar exude a profound retro charm, each piece of furniture and decoration a witness to bygone days, recounting the city's past glory.

Here, time seems to stand still. Patrons lounge on comfortable sofas or lean against the bar, savoring bespoke cocktails or sipping on rich coffee aromas. Attendants dressed in retro attire, their smiles reflecting a deep appreciation and respect for the era, pay homage to every drink served. As music gradually fills the air, the cafe transforms into a small dance floor, lights dancing on the walls, creating a mysterious and enchanting ambiance. Here, people unwind, sharing their stories, as if collectively weaving small yet beautiful moments.

PikaPika cafe and bar is not just a nightspot; it's a gateway to a time tunnel, offering an escape from the fast pace of modern life, allowing a rediscovery of refinement and leisure, and a taste of the unique charm of the night.
